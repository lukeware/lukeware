About Me

Hello World, my name is Luke Ware and I’m someone who loves teaching and learning. I currently work in higher education managing an online course design lab where I also host design workshops and teach teachers about copyright, open educational resources, and tech tools to help make learning better. I’m trained in modern frontend development workflows, and I use GitHub whenever possible as I believe in open and transparent access to code. I’m looking to collaborate with others on great projects and to learn as much as I can about both front and back end development.
